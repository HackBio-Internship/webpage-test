What is HackBio Institute?

HackBio Academy is a paid virtual bioinformatics campus learning program that brings together scientists from different walks of life to a collaborative workspace to solve important biological questions. The workspace for collaboration is quite different from that of the internship. We built the workspace to "help" you. It is perfectly suits most learners irrespective of your scientific background, and makes you focus on strengthening your weakness. While learning is at your own pace, you are time-bound. It is a mentored experience. Top scientists from Industry and Academia are on board to always help you. We are fast and we make sure you have an academic publication to show for your experience with us. We also strive to provide financial incentives and connect beautiful career opportunities through our Connectivity Hub. It is an incredible opportunity to learn, earn, network, collaborate, publish and get your dream job with our partners. Trust us, you have been waiting for this. See the numbers below.

Our Plan

Study at the HackBio Institute is completely virtual, i.e., you can earn our certificate at the comfort of your home or office. The entire study is divided into 3 academic phases: Classroom, Gym and Innovation. Each phase must be completed within one month. 

1

Classroom

Introduction to theoretical concepts and background to molecular biology and programming languages.
2

Gym

Paper practicals and seminars. Each student completely replicates research performed in at least 2 peer-reviewed paper and presents it at the end of two weeks.
3

Innovation

Perform a research project that solves key challenges faced by HackBio Partners. In collaboration with HackBio partners, you will be provided with all resources and reasonable stipend sufficient for the period of research.
