What is HackBio Internship?

HackBio Internship is an open virtual bioinformatics internship that brings together scientists from different walks of life to a collaborative workspace to solve important biological questions. Although we have had situations whereby novices were able to complete the internship, we iterate that the internship is not a classical do-as-I-do training and may not be suitable if this is your first time of attempting bioinformatics. It is a mentored experience. We are fast and we make sure you have an academic publication to show for your experience with us. We also strive to provide financial incentives when you solve important biological tasks. It is an incredible opportunity to learn, earn, network, collaborate and publish. DONATE

HackBio internship is a community event. It is 100% open and depends on kind donations from companies, scientists, laboratories and agencies. All donations are used to support scientists while they develop solutions in 3 ways:

  • Provide financial Incentive to top problem solvers;
  • Provide internet supply for the period;  
  • Provide support for women and underrepresented groups;
  • Software/Tool licensing support.
