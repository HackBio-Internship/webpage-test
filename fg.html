Molecular Biology

An open-minded introduction to the central dogma with an emphasis on the understanding the core concepts and their use of these concepts for problem solving.

Programming for Life Scientists

Practice oriented introduction to the core programming languages in life science; Python, R and Bash. Data Visualization and App development primers are integrated to give you an edge.

Statistics and Probability

Elementary introduction to the theories of statistics and probabilities, as well as a quick advance into practical applications in bioinformatics and computing.

Molecular Docking

Structure based prediction of drug-receptor interactions; virtual phytochemical extraction, high throughput screening for large scale target identification projects.

Quantitative Structure Activity Relationship  (QSAR)

Machine learning guided approach to optimizing drug candidates using QSAR/QSPR methods as well as ML-based prediction of bioactivity (IC50).

Biochemical Data Visualization

Interactive visualization, annotation and analysis of drug-receptor interactions. Publication-quality production of scientific images.

Biomarker/Target Identification

Identification of disease biomarkers and drug targets through an integrated transcriptomics approach.
